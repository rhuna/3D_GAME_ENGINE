# V79 Encounter Authoring Suite

V79 adds a new Fight tab to GameBuilderPanel for low-code enemy archetypes, encounter graph assets, and encounter bundles.

## New outputs
- assets/enemies/<archetype>.enemy
- assets/encountergraphs/<encounter>.encountergraph
- assets/links/<encounter>_encounter.links
