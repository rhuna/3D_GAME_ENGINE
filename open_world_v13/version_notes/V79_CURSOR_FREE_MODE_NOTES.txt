V79 - Free Cursor Builder Camera Mode

What changed
- Builder mode no longer ties cursor movement to camera look.
- When the visual builder is open, the cursor stays free by default.
- To orbit the camera while the builder is open, hold Alt + Right Mouse Button.
- Releasing Alt or RMB restores the cursor to its original menu position.
- Closing the builder also restores any temporary orbit state.

Patched files
- src/core/Application.h
- src/core/Application.cpp

How to use
- F8 opens/closes the visual builder.
- Move the mouse freely in the builder panel.
- Hold Alt + RMB to temporarily orbit the camera.
- Use E / Q while orbiting to move camera up / down, matching existing controls.
